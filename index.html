Topics
Topics implement the publish/subscribe model of messaging. Many listeners may be subscribed to a topic at once, and all may receive the message. This is a pattern well-suited to the broadcast of information. If a listener is unsubscribed at the time a message is sent to the topic, it will not receive that particular message.

Queues
Queues define a point-to-point messaging model. There may be many listeners upon a queue, but a message will only be processed by one of them. Messages remain on the queue until processed.
